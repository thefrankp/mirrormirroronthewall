Privacy Policy

Mirror Mirror On The Wall — Last updated: 31 May 2026

Mirror Mirror On The Wall (“the App”) is developed and published by FP TECH. This policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used. The short version: we collect nothing.

Camera Access

The App uses your device’s front-facing camera to display a live video feed on your screen. All processing happens entirely on your device. No images, video, or any camera data are ever recorded, stored, uploaded, or transmitted anywhere.

Date & Time

The App periodically contacts an FP Tech server to retrieve the current date and time for display on screen. These requests contain no personal information whatsoever — only a standard network request is made.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share anything. None of these:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any images or video from your camera

Third Parties

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@fptech.com.au.
